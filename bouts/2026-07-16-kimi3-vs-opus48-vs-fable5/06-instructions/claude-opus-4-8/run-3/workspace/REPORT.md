## Overview

Across 30 orders placed between May 2 and May 21, 2026, total revenue reached $7,154.44. The data spans four regions and four products, giving an average order value of about $238.

## Top Products

Gadget led all products at $2,392.00, earning the most revenue from just nine orders thanks to its $149.50 price. Widget followed at $2,118.94 across many smaller sales. Doohickey brought in $1,513.00, and Sprocket contributed $1,130.50 on high volume but a low $4.75 price.

## Regional Performance

North was the strongest region at $2,176.35, followed by East at $1,830.79 and South at $1,732.48. West trailed at $1,414.82. The gap between the top and bottom regions is roughly $761, showing uneven demand.

## Recommendations

- Prioritize Gadget inventory and promotion; at $149.50 per unit it earns the most revenue from the fewest orders.
- Support West with targeted campaigns; at $1,414.82 it trails North by $761 and shows the clearest room to grow.
- Bundle low-price, high-volume Sprocket with premium items to raise its $1,130.50 total, currently the weakest product despite strong unit counts.
