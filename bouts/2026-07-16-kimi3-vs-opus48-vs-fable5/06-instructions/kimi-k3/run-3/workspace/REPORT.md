## Overview

The dataset covers 30 orders placed between 2026-05-02 and 2026-05-21 across
four regions and four products. Total revenue was $7,154.44, with a median
order value of $204.94.

## Top Products

Gadget generated the most revenue at $2,392.00 from only 16 units sold,
thanks to its $149.50 unit price. Widget followed at $2,118.94 on much higher
volume (106 units). Doohickey brought in $1,513.00, while Sprocket trailed at
$1,130.50 despite the highest unit count (198 units) because of its low
$4.75 price.

## Regional Performance

North led all regions with $2,176.35 in revenue. East followed at $1,830.79,
then South at $1,732.48. West was the weakest region at $1,414.82, roughly
35% below North. North combined strong Widget and Sprocket volume with
steady Gadget sales.

## Recommendations

- Prioritize Gadget sales efforts in every region, since it delivers the highest revenue per unit sold.
- Investigate why West underperforms other regions and test targeted promotions to lift its order volume.
- Review Sprocket pricing, as its high unit sales contribute little revenue compared with other products.
