## Overview

This report summarizes 30 orders placed between 2026-05-02 and 2026-05-21 across four regions and four products. Total revenue was $7,154.44, with an average order value of $238.48 and a median of $204.94. Individual orders ranged from $47.50 to $598.00.

## Top Products

Gadget generated the most revenue at $2,392.00 from only 16 units sold. Widget followed with $2,118.94 on 106 units. Doohickey contributed $1,513.00 from 17 units, while Sprocket earned $1,130.50 on 238 units — the largest unit count but the lowest revenue per unit by far.

## Regional Performance

North led all regions with $2,176.35 in revenue. East followed at $1,830.79, South at $1,732.48, and West trailed at $1,414.82. The gap between the top and bottom regions is $761.53, roughly 19% of the regional revenue total.

## Recommendations

- Promote Gadget more widely: only 16 units sold yet it produced $2,392.00, the highest product revenue.
- Invest in West, the weakest region at $1,414.82, with targeted campaigns to close the gap with North.
- Keep Sprocket as a volume driver but review its pricing, since 238 units returned only $1,130.50.
