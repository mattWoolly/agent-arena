## Overview

This report covers 30 orders placed between 2026-05-02 and 2026-05-21, generating total revenue of $7,154.44. The median order value was $204.94. Sales span four products (Widget, Gadget, Sprocket, Doohickey) and four regions (North, South, East, West).

## Top Products

Gadget leads with $2,392.00 in revenue from only 16 units, thanks to its $149.50 unit price. Widget follows at $2,118.94 on 106 units. Doohickey earned $1,513.00 from 17 units, while Sprocket, despite moving the most volume (238 units), brought in just $1,130.50 at its low $4.75 price point.

## Regional Performance

North is the strongest region at $2,176.35, followed by East at $1,830.79 and South at $1,732.48. West trails at $1,414.82, about 35% below North. The gap between the top and bottom regions is roughly $760, so no single region dominates, but West clearly underperforms its peers.

## Recommendations

- Prioritize Gadget promotion: it earns the most revenue per unit sold, so small volume gains would meaningfully lift total revenue.
- Investigate West's shortfall versus North; replicating North's product mix or sales tactics there could close the roughly $760 regional gap.
- Reassess Sprocket pricing or bundling: 238 units yielded only $1,130.50, the weakest revenue despite the highest sales volume.
