## Overview

This report covers 30 orders recorded in data/sales.csv between 2026-05-02 and 2026-05-21, spanning four regions (North, South, East, West) and four products. Total revenue was $7,154.44 across those 30 orders, averaging $238.48 per order with a median order value of $204.94.

## Top Products

Gadget led all products with $2,392.00 in revenue from only 16 units, driven by its $149.50 unit price. Widget followed at $2,118.94 and appeared in 10 of the 30 orders, more than any other product. Doohickey earned $1,513.00 from 17 units. Sprocket earned the least, $1,130.50, despite moving 238 units — by far the highest volume — because of its $4.75 price.

## Regional Performance

North led with $2,176.35 in revenue, followed by East at $1,830.79 and South at $1,732.48. West trailed at $1,414.82, about 65% of North's total. Every region purchased all four products, but West placed only one Gadget order, while each other region placed two.

## Recommendations

- Focus sales effort on West, the weakest region ($1,414.82); it placed only one Gadget order versus two in every other region.
- Review Sprocket pricing: 238 units sold, the highest volume, produced only $1,130.50, the lowest product revenue at $4.75 per unit.
- Promote high-priced Gadget ($149.50) more widely; just 16 units generated $2,392.00, the top product revenue, so small volume gains matter.
