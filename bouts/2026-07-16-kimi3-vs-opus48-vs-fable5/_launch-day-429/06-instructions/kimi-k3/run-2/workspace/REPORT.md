## Overview

Total revenue across 30 orders was $7,154.44. The median order value was
$204.94. Sales span four regions and four products, with revenue spread
fairly evenly across the catalog.

## Top Products

Gadget is the top product at $2,392.00 in revenue, followed by Widget at
$2,118.94. Doohickey earned $1,513.00 and Sprocket earned $1,130.50. The
top two products together account for about 63% of total revenue.

## Regional Performance

North leads all regions with $2,176.35, followed by East at $1,830.79 and
South at $1,732.48. West trails the others at $1,414.82, roughly 20% of
total revenue and the weakest region overall.

## Recommendations

- Increase Gadget stock and promotion in the North, where regional revenue is already highest.
- Investigate why West lags other regions; test targeted discounts or expanded product availability there.
- Bundle Sprocket with Gadget or Widget to lift the lowest-earning product's revenue share.
