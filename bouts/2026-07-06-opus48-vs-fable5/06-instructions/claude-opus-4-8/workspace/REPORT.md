## Overview

Total revenue across the dataset was $7,154.44 from 30 orders placed between
May 2 and May 21, 2026. The median order value was $204.94, reflecting a mix
of high-value, low-unit products and low-value, high-unit products.

## Top Products

Gadget led all products at $2,392.00 in revenue, driven by its $149.50 unit
price despite modest unit counts. Widget followed at $2,118.94, earning through
high order frequency and volume. Doohickey contributed $1,513.00 and Sprocket
$1,130.50. Together, Gadget and Widget accounted for roughly 63% of total
revenue.

## Regional Performance

North was the strongest region at $2,176.35, followed by East at $1,830.79 and
South at $1,732.48. West trailed at $1,414.82. The gap between the top and
bottom region was about $761, indicating uneven demand distribution across the
four territories during the period.

## Recommendations

- Prioritize Gadget inventory and marketing, since it generates the most
  revenue per order despite low unit volume.
- Investigate West region underperformance; its $1,414.82 total is the lowest
  and roughly 35% below North.
- Bundle high-volume Widget and Sprocket sales with premium products to raise
  the $204.94 median order value.
