## Overview

Sales data covers 30 orders placed between May 2 and May 21, 2026, spanning four products and four regions. Total revenue was $7,154.44, averaging $238.48 per order with a median order value of $204.94.

## Top Products

Gadget led all products with $2,392.00 in revenue from just 16 units, driven by its $149.50 unit price. Widget followed at $2,118.94 (106 units), then Doohickey at $1,513.00 (17 units) and Sprocket at $1,130.50. Sprocket moved by far the most units (238) yet earned the least revenue, while the two premium-priced products (Gadget and Doohickey) generated 55% of total revenue from only 33 units.

## Regional Performance

North was the strongest region at $2,176.35, about 30% of total revenue. East followed at $1,830.79 and South at $1,732.48. West trailed at $1,414.82 — roughly 65% of North's total — making it the clearest opportunity for improvement.

## Recommendations

- Prioritize Gadget and Doohickey promotion: their high unit prices mean small volume gains add outsized revenue, as 33 units already produced $3,905.00.
- Investigate West's shortfall ($1,414.82, lowest of all regions) and test targeted campaigns there to close the $761.53 gap with North.
- Revisit Sprocket pricing or bundling: 238 units sold returned only $1,130.50, the weakest product revenue despite the highest sales volume.
