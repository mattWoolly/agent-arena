## Overview

This analysis covers 30 orders with total revenue of $5,288.55. Orders span five weeks across four regions and four product categories.

## Top Products

Widget leads revenue generation with $2,269.15 (43% of total), driven by volume across all regions. Gadget follows with $1,043.00, then Doohickey at $890.00 and Sprocket at $1,086.40.

## Regional Performance

North generated $1,408.28, the highest regional revenue. South contributed $1,138.31, East $1,155.49, and West $586.47. North's strength stems from high-volume Widget and Sprocket orders.

## Recommendations

- Focus Widget distribution in North and South regions where demand proves highest across multiple order dates.
- Expand Gadget availability; strong per-unit pricing justifies inventory investment despite lower order frequency.
- Investigate West underperformance; region represents only 11% of revenue and warrants targeted promotion or pricing review.
