## Overview

Total revenue was $7,154.44 across 30 orders. Revenue spans four product
lines and four regions, with clear differences in contribution across both
products and regions.

## Top Products

Gadget led all products with $2,392.00 in revenue, followed by Widget at
$2,118.94, Doohickey at $1,513.00, and Sprocket at $1,130.50. Gadget's high
unit price drove its lead despite fewer units sold than Widget or Sprocket.

## Regional Performance

North was the top region at $2,176.35, followed by East at $1,830.79, South
at $1,732.48, and West at $1,414.82. North outsold West by roughly 54%,
pointing to uneven demand or coverage across regions.

## Recommendations

- Prioritize Gadget stock and promotion given its outsized revenue versus units sold.
- Study why West lags other regions and test targeted promotions to close the gap.
- Review Sprocket pricing since its high unit volume yields comparatively low revenue.
