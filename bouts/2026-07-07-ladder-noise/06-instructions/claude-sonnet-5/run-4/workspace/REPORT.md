## Overview

Total revenue across all orders was $7,154.44 from 30 orders, averaging about $238 per order over the period May 2 to May 21, 2026.

## Top Products

Gadget generated the most revenue ($2,392.00) despite only 16 units sold, driven by its $149.50 unit price. Widget followed with $2,118.94 from 106 units. Doohickey earned $1,513.00, and Sprocket, despite selling the most units (238), earned the least revenue ($1,130.50) due to its low $4.75 price.

North led all regions with $2,176.35 in revenue across 8 orders. East followed with $1,830.79 from 7 orders, then South with $1,732.48 from 8 orders. West trailed with $1,414.82 from 7 orders, the lowest of any region.

## Recommendations

- Push Gadget promotions in West, the lowest-revenue region, to close the gap with North.
- Review why West and East generate less per order than North despite similar order counts.
