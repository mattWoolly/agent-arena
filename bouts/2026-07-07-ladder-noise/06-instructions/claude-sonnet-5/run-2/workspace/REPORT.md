## Overview

Total revenue across the period was $7,154.44 from 30 orders, spanning four products (Widget, Gadget, Sprocket, Doohickey) and four regions (North, South, East, West). The median order value was $204.94.

## Top Products

Gadget produced the most revenue ($2,392.00) from only 16 units across 7 orders, thanks to its high $149.50 unit price. Widget ranked second ($2,118.94) with the most orders (10) and 106 units sold. Doohickey brought in $1,513.00, and Sprocket, despite selling the most units (238), generated the least revenue ($1,130.50) because of its low $4.75 price point.

## Regional Performance

North was the strongest region, generating $2,176.35 from 142 units across 8 orders. East followed with $1,830.79, South close behind at $1,732.48, and West trailed all regions at $1,414.82 from just 55 units, the lowest volume observed.

## Recommendations

- Promote Gadget more heavily since its high price drives outsized revenue from relatively few orders.
- Investigate West's low unit volume; targeted promotions there could close the gap with North.
- Consider a modest price increase on Sprocket, as high unit sales currently yield comparatively little revenue.
