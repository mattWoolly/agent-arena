## Overview

This report covers 30 orders recorded between May 2 and May 21, 2026, across four regions and four products. Total revenue was $7,154.44. The median order value was $204.94 and the average was $238.48.

## Top Products

Gadget generated the most revenue at $2,392.00 from only 16 units, reflecting its high unit price. Widget followed at $2,118.94 on 106 units, then Doohickey at $1,513.00. Sprocket earned the least, $1,130.50, despite selling 238 units — the highest volume of any product.

## Regional Performance

North led all regions with $2,176.35 in revenue, followed by East at $1,830.79 and South at $1,732.48. West trailed at $1,414.82, roughly 35% below North.

## Recommendations

- Promote Gadget more widely: it earns the most revenue from the fewest units, so small volume gains have outsized impact.
- Investigate West's underperformance ($1,414.82, last among regions) and test targeted campaigns to close the gap with North.
- Review Sprocket pricing; 238 units sold produced only $1,130.50, the lowest product revenue despite the highest volume.
