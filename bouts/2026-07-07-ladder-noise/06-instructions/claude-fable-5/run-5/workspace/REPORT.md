## Overview

Total revenue for the period 2026-05-02 through 2026-05-21 was $7,154.44 across 30 orders. The median order value was $204.94, spanning four products (Widget, Gadget, Sprocket, Doohickey) and four regions (North, South, East, West).

## Top Products

Gadget leads with $2,392.00 in revenue from only 16 units, reflecting its high unit price. Widget follows at $2,118.94 on 106 units, the strongest volume seller among premium-priced items. Doohickey earned $1,513.00 from 17 units, while Sprocket, despite moving 238 units, generated just $1,130.50 due to its low price point.

## Regional Performance

North is the top region at $2,176.35, followed by East at $1,830.79 and South at $1,732.48. West trails at $1,414.82, roughly 65% of North's total. Revenue is fairly balanced overall, with no single region contributing more than about 30% of the total.

## Recommendations

- Prioritize Gadget promotion: it earns the most revenue per unit sold, so small volume gains yield outsized returns.
- Investigate West's underperformance ($1,414.82, the lowest region) to close its gap with North ($2,176.35).
- Bundle low-priced Sprockets with Widgets to lift Sprocket revenue, which lags despite the highest unit volume (238 units).
