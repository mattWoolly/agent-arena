## Overview

Total revenue across all orders was $7,154.44, generated from 30 orders placed between 2026-05-02 and 2026-05-20. The median order value was $204.94, showing a fairly even spread between large and small purchases.

## Top Products

Gadget led with $2,392.00 in revenue, followed by Widget at $2,118.94. Doohickey earned $1,513.00 and Sprocket brought in $1,130.50. Gadget's high unit price drove its lead despite lower unit counts.

## Regional Performance

North was the strongest region at $2,176.35, ahead of East ($1,830.79) and South ($1,732.48). West trailed at $1,414.82, about 35% below North.

## Recommendations

- Focus promotion on Gadget and Widget, the two top earners, since they together produced over 60% of total revenue.
- Investigate the West region, the lowest performer, and apply lessons from the stronger North results.
- Review pricing for Sprocket, the lowest-revenue product, to see if volume growth can lift its contribution.
